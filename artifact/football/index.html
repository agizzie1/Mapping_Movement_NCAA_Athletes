2025–26 Transfer Portal Flow

Each ring is a full circle of conferences, subdivided into schools.

Outer ring

The outer ring represents NCAA member schools and conferences, sized by a flat 105-player roster limit. Within a conference, neighboring schools are shaded slightly lighter or darker than each other purely so adjacent schools stay visually distinguishable — that shade variation doesn't encode any data of its own.

Inner ring

The inner ring sizes each school by how many of its players actually entered the transfer portal. The muted gray segments represent players who entered the portal but, as of this scrape, hadn't transferred elsewhere at this level (still searching, or moved to the other level / outside FBS & FCS).

Functions

Zoom into a school's inner-ring segment to split it from one solid block into the individual players behind it — hover one to see just their own ribbon, or click a colored or gray inner-ring segment (at any zoom) to list every player in it, name and transfer date included, in the side panel on the right.

Hover a conference label to see that conference's transfers in and out.

Hover a school on the inner ring to trace its individual transfers — ribbons are colored by the conference the player left.

Click a conference, school, or (once zoomed in) an individual player line to pin its ribbons in place; click it again, or click empty space, to release it.

The Out / In / Both toggle narrows a hover or pin to just outgoing transfers, just incoming, or both at once.

Filters

The Filters bar below each diagram's controls adds Conference, School, Position, Class, Prior-transfers, and Transfer-date chips — picking any of them immediately draws every matching ribbon across the whole diagram (no hovering required), dims every non-matching player tick, and shows a live match count. Chips within one row are additive (Sophomore + Junior shows both); rows combine narrower (Sophomore and Cornerback). The Conference and School filters match a player's home conference/school, so picking SEC + Cornerback shows exactly where SEC cornerbacks are transferring to. Every ribbon a filter, hover, or pin puts on screen is clickable — click one to list the players behind it, filters included, in the side panel.
