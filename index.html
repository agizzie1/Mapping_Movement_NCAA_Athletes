Mapping Movement: NCAA Athletes

About Football Basketball

The Project

This tool was created to depict the movement of NCAA D1 Football and Men's Basketball student-athletes in the context of an ever-evolving college NIL landscape in Division 1 sports.

With athletes moving between schools and conferences in search of higher paying deals and no cap on the number of times a player can enter the NCAA transfer portal, tracking student-athlete movement is essential to understanding the impact of NIL since July 2025 on student-athlete experiences.
